VP Supply Chain – Kohler Kitchen & Bath (2021–2023)

Role Overview

As Vice President of Supply Chain for Kohler Kitchen & Bath North America, I managed global supply chain operations, overseeing $3.5B in revenue, $550M in supply chain costs, $576M in inventory, and 12 distribution centers. My focus was on operational excellence, inventory optimization, and driving sustainable growth.

Key Achievements

  • Improved OTIF (On-Time In-Full) from 62% to 86% within two years.
  • Reduced past-due orders from 18 days to 3 days on average.
  • Increased warehouse productivity by 33.6% while supporting $1B revenue growth.
  • Reduced supply chain costs from 11.5% to 9.8% of sales.
  • Implemented SAP-IBP and advanced analytics for proactive supply chain management.
